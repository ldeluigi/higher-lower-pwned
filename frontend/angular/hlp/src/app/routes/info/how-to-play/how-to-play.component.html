How To Play

Arcade

Arcade Mode is a single-player survival game where you are put in front of two passwords, the first has a popularity score, while the second one doesn't. You must choose and select which of the two passwords has the higher popularity score before the time runs out.

Duel

Duel Mode is a multi-player survival game much like Arcade, but your passwords are also given to your opponent. The first of the two that selects an answer must wait for the other, while his or her timer is paused. When someone loses, either from a wrong answer or a timeout, the other wins, and can keep playing in solo just to make a new best score in that mode. The loser can still see his or her opponent playing with Spectator Mode. In multi-player modes, the maximum available time that can be reached is set to 30 seconds.

Battle Royale

Battle Royale works similar to Duel, but instead of only one opponent there are 99.
